Projects

Find My Doughnuts

Using the Google Maps API, Find My Doughnuts will try and find doughnut stores near you.

If supported, the app will use the location of your device using Geolocation to find doughnuts at a click of a button. If not, you can search by a location (address, landmark, etc) instead. This is a great way to plan ahead and find stores where you are already heading!

browserprefixes

Using a cheat sheet for CSS prefixes can make frontend development that much easier; remembering them for IE, Firefox and Chrome can be a handful. Having a list of them within arm's reach at all times will save you time that would have otherwise been spent tearing your hair out as to why your Flexbox system is not respecting your content in that one browser.
